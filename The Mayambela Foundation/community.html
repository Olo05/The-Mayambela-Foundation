Gogo Nobude Ngqola

her family in Site B

The elderly Ngqola has been living in Site B since 1987 and has never owned a house ever since then. Living in the densely-populated informal dwelling has been full of challenges for Ngqola as she battles to support her eight children and grandchildren with her old-age grant.

But on Friday she got some relief when soccer players Mark Mayambela and Ayanda Patosi arrived at her shack with groceries for her and her family. Overcome with emotion, the old woman wept uncontrollably. “May the Lord bless you abundantly my children.

No one has ever done something like this for me,” she said. Ngqola was among 10 families who benefited from the generous donation of the two former Cape Town City FC soccer stars. The families were identified by ward 90 councillor Lulekwa Jali after she was approached by the Mayambela Foundation.

Both products of Khayelitsha

Mayambela and Patosi

went on to become household names in South African football despite hailing from poor backgrounds.
